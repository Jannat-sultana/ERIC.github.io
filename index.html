Research Wings

ERIC is organized into specialized research wings, each focusing on different domains of scientific inquiry and innovation.

Computational Sciences Wing

Focuses on algorithm development, high-performance computing, and computational modeling across various scientific disciplines.

Biotechnology & Life Sciences Wing

Dedicated to advancing research in molecular biology, genetics, bioengineering, and medical technologies.

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning Wing

Developing cutting-edge AI solutions, neural networks, and intelligent systems for real-world applications.

Environmental Sciences Wing

Research focused on sustainability, climate change, renewable energy, and environmental conservation technologies.

Social Sciences & Policy Wing

Interdisciplinary research examining societal impacts of technology and evidence-based policy development.
